Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of account connection data and server-stored ClipQue data.

How to request deletion

  1. Email jawadeeni8@gmail.com with the subject line “ClipQue Data Deletion Request”.
  2. Include the TikTok username or account identifier connected to ClipQue, if available.
  3. We may ask for reasonable verification before deleting account-linked data.

What can be deleted

Deletion may include OAuth tokens, account identifiers, server-side upload queue records, and support records where retention is not legally required.

Local files

ClipQue desktop files stored on your device, such as clips, captions, and project folders, can be deleted directly from your device by you.

Timing

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days unless a longer period is required by law, security, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution.
