Privacy Policy

Effective date:

This Privacy Policy explains how ClipQue collects, uses, stores, and protects information when users access our website or connect their TikTok account to ClipQue features.

1. Who we are

ClipQue is a creator workflow tool that helps users prepare short-form video clips, captions, hashtags, and upload-ready packages. ClipQue is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by TikTok.

2. Information we may collect

  • Website information: basic technical data such as browser type, device type, pages visited, and approximate region may be collected by our hosting provider for security and service operation.
  • Account connection data: when a user authorizes TikTok access, TikTok may return authorization data such as an authorization code, access token, refresh token, token expiry, granted scopes, and basic creator/account identifiers permitted by the user.
  • User content data: if users enable upload or posting features, ClipQue may process video file names, clip metadata, captions, hashtags, posting status, and related upload information needed to complete the user-requested workflow.
  • Support data: if users contact us, we may receive their name, email address, message content, and related troubleshooting details.

3. Information we do not intentionally collect

ClipQue is not designed to collect sensitive personal information such as government identification numbers, precise location, health information, financial account details, or biometric identifiers. Users should not submit sensitive information through ClipQue support channels.

4. How we use information

  • To provide, operate, and improve ClipQue features.
  • To authenticate user-authorized TikTok connections through OAuth.
  • To prepare uploads, captions, hashtags, and posting workflows requested by the user.
  • To troubleshoot errors, protect the service, prevent abuse, and respond to support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations and platform requirements.

5. TikTok OAuth and permissions

ClipQue only requests TikTok permissions needed for the features the user chooses to use. Users must authorize access through TikTok’s OAuth flow. Users can revoke access through TikTok account settings or by contacting ClipQue support.

6. User control and posting responsibility

Users remain responsible for their videos, captions, hashtags, account activity, and compliance with applicable laws and platform rules. ClipQue is designed to support user-approved workflows and does not require users to post any specific content.

7. Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only with service providers needed to operate ClipQue, with TikTok when users connect their account or request uploads/posting, when required by law, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

8. Data retention

We retain information only as long as needed to provide the service, complete user-requested workflows, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our Terms. Local desktop files created by the user remain on the user’s device unless the user uploads them through a connected service.

9. Data deletion

Users may request deletion of account connection data and server-stored data by following the instructions on our Data Deletion page. Local files stored on a user’s own device can be deleted by the user directly.

10. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. No system is completely secure, and users should protect their own devices, passwords, and connected accounts.

11. Children

ClipQue is not intended for children under the age required to use TikTok or under the age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. International use

Users may access ClipQue from different regions. Information may be processed where our service providers operate, subject to applicable law.

13. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
