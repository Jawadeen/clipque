Clip preparation

Caption support

User control

Users choose what content they process, review captions, and decide what is uploaded or posted.

Responsible posting workflow

ClipQue is designed for creator-owned or properly licensed content. The tool does not claim ownership of user videos and is not affiliated with TikTok.

  • Users must only process and upload content they own or are authorized to use.
  • Users remain responsible for reviewing all captions, hashtags, and video content before posting.
  • Any TikTok connection uses OAuth authorization and requires user permission.
  • Users can disconnect access and request deletion of stored account data if server-side features are enabled.
